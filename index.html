What do we do?

Maps are a vital tool for every occupation and everyday life. It provides a way for us to describes spatial information. From local government to satellite observation programs, there more and more spatial data available on the Internet. It enables us to produce analyses and maps with lower and lower costs.

Raumgeist is a website that creates anything spatial, from maps to satellite images, to provide a new perspective of the world from open data.
